Berlin Free Photowalk

by Sergey Poliakov

We are going to set a walk. Preferably in the district, that you like the most - it can be your own hood, some place in Berlin, that really inspires you. If you don’t have any favourites - I am going to give you suggestions.

We are going to walk for a couple of hours and I am going to take your pictures. I want to make pictures very lively and natural, so posing will be very limited, mostly it is going to be a reportage technique.

After the photowalk I am going to pick around 100-120 best pictures for «your album» (I am going to upload it to google drive and you can use it for whatever you want) and around 10-12 pictures will go to my site and portfolio. I’ll try to edit them as soon as I can. You are going to receive them in 1,2 month the latest.

The photowalk is totally free. I was an art and street photographer back in Saint-Petersburg and since I moved here I want to get back to the photowalks/wedding/fashion/portraits thing.

Below there are free spots (green is free, red is busy, «orange» are the days I am not sure about).  If they don’t suit you - let’s set a date personally, just write me 3/4 time slots during next 2/3 weeks, that suit you best.
