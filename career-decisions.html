Should I stay or should I go? You can catch yourself thinking about it after another farewell party, good-bye email, 1:1 with your manager, company update or looking at how your new coworker watching movies at work. Having experience in different jobs before IT in this talk, I will raise questions of happiness and self-fulfilment at the working space, the possibilities to have open conversations about it with your management and supervisors, how to find the courage to try to change something before handling your resignation and why "just quit" is not always a meaningful statement.
