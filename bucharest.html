First impressions of Bucharest

by Sergey Poliakov

With my pictures I’m trying to show the beauty of everyday urban life. I’m offering my captured subjects to create a little pause in their daily rush, look around and find the hidden beauty, which is surrounding every moment.

During the last weeks I was having long walks with my camera in Bucharest, where beauty is hidden everywhere - the details of buildings, the secret gardens, when people enjoying their summertime. Those are my first photo-impressions of the town - people, busy with their lives, places and architecture.

Sergey Poliakov
Street photographer and designer from Saint-Petersburg, Russia.
